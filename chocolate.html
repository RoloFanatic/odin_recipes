Chocolate cake

Paragraph about the cake...

Ingredients

    Cake

  • Self rasising flour 225g
  • Caster sugar 350g
  • Cocoa powder 85g
  • Baking powder 1.5 tsp
  • Bicarbonate of soda 1.5 tsp
  • Eggs 2 medium
  • Milk 250 ml
  • Vegetable oil 125ml
  • Vanilla extract 2tsp
  • Boiling water 250ml

    • Gnache

  • Dark chocholate 200g
  • Double cream 200ml

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180C/160C fan
  2. Mix together all of the cake ingredients in a large mixing bowl EXCEPT for the boiling water.
  3. Add the boiling water in slowly with mixing until well combined and smooth (the mixture will be very runny).
  4. Divide the mixture in two and pour into sealed cake tins lined with butter (or grease-proof paper if you are lazy like me).
  5. Bake for 25-35 mins until a skewer inserted into the middle of the cake comes out clean.
  6. Remove from oven and allow them to cool completely.
  7. Whilst the cakes are cooling; Gently heat the cream and chocolate over a water bath until all of the chocolate dissolves. Remove from the heat and allow to cool at room temperature.
  8. Once the cakes are cooled, and the ganache has thickened to a syrup like consistency (or a little thicker), add a portion to the top of one of the cakes before staking them. Pour the rest of the ganache over the cake and smooth it over the top and sides with a knife.
  9. This cake must be kept in the fridge due to the ganache coating. Keeps for 4-5 days.
