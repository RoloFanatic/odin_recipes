Lemon Drizzle cake

Paragraph about the cake...

Ingredients

    Cake

  • Self rasising flour 225g
  • Caster sugar 225g
  • Unsalted butter 225g
  • Lemon zest 1 lemon
  • Eggs 4 medium

    • Drizzle

  • Lemon juice 3 lemons
  • Caster sugar 170g

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180C/160C fan
  2. Mix together the butter and caster sugar until creamy consistency is achieved. Add in the eggs one at a time and mix in slowly.
  3. Sift in the self-raising flour, then add lemon zest and mix thoroughly.
  4. Grease the muffin tray with a little butter, then spoon the mixture into each case until about 2/3 full.
  5. Bake for approximately 15 mins or until a skewer inserted comes out clean.
  6. Remove from oven and allow it to begin cooling.
  7. Whilst the cakes are cooling; Gently heat the sugar and lemon juice mixture over the hob until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  8. Whilst the cakes are still warm, cut off the central portion of each cupcake (like you would with a butterfly cake) and spoon 1.5 tsp of the syrup into each cupcake. Replace the top of the cake and drizzle the remaining syrup over all of the cupcakes.
  9. These cupcakes will keep for 3-4 days in an air tight container.
