Apple crumble

Paragraph about the cake...

Ingredients

    Filling

  • Bramley apples, 3
  • Golden caster sugar 2tbsp
  • Cinnamon 1tbsp
  • Water 2tbsp

    • Crumble

  • Plaing flour 175g
  • Demerara sugar 110g
  • Unsalted (cold) butter 110g

    • Topping (optional)

  • Rolled oats 1tbsp
  • Demerara sugar 1tbsp
  • Serve with cold cream or custard.

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven to 170C fan
  2. Roughly chop the apples into 2cm cubes.
  3. Gently heat the apples in a saucepan with the cinnamon and water (optional: add 1tbsp sugar if the apples too tart).
  4. Once the apples start to becomme soft (5-10 mins) remove the pan from the heat and allow it to cool.
  5. Prepare the crumble by using your fingertips to bredcrumb the flour and sugar together with the cold butter.
  6. Pour the apple mix into a ceramic or pyrex baking dish and cover with the crumble.
  7. Sprinkle over the oat and sugar mixture.
  8. Bake for 35-40 mins. Eat while hot with cold custard for best results.
