Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter 145g
  • Demerara sugar 100g
  • Caster sugar 125g
  • Eggs 1 medium
  • Vanilla extract 1tsp
  • Self-raising flour 225g
  • Salt 0.5tsp
  • Chocolate chips 200g

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven to 160C fan
  2. Mix butter and sugar together until creamy consistency is achieved, then add in the egg and vanilla.
  3. Sift in the flour, salt and finally the chocolate.
  4. Roll the dough into golfball sized balls for large cookies, or walnuts for more modestly sized cookies.
  5. Place the balls on a baking tray lined with baking paper.
  6. Bake for around 10 mins, or until golden.
  7. Allow the cookies to cool.
